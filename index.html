About Me

Software Engineer, FullStack Developer, and a Engineer by Heart

I am currently a 3rd-year Computer Science student at the University of British Columbia. Looking back, my journey here wasn’t always smooth. In elementary school, I almost got in trouble for selling products to classmates—a young entrepreneurial spark that didn’t quite align with school rules.

High school came with its own challenges. I struggled and failed several courses, including Chemistry 11, Math 12, and Physics 11 and 12, but instead of being discouraged by those setbacks, I used them as motivation. I discovered my real strengths in programming, where I could apply my problem-solving skills in ways that felt more natural to me.

Now, I’m proficient in programming languages like Python, TypeScript, C++, and many more. What started as a curiosity has become a passion for building software and tackling complex problems through technology. While my path hasn’t been traditional, it’s shaped who I am today, and I’m excited to keep learning and growing in the field.
